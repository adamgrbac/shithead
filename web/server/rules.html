Rules for Shithead

  • The first player to have no cards wins.
  • A player may only play from their lowest row of cards i.e. if they have cards in their hand, they may not place from their 3 face-up cards
  • To play a card, it must be equal or higher than the previous card, unless other rules below apply.
  • You may play multiple cards of the same value in one turn e.g 6H & 6D.
  • If 4 cards of the same value are played consecutively, the player of the final card discards the pile and plays again
  • 2 - Reset: If a 2 is played, the next player may play whatever card they like. A 2 may be played on any card.
  • 3 - Glass: If a 3 is played, the next player must play to whatever the card was underneath the 3 i.e. 3 on a 5 means the next player plays according to the 5. A 3 may be played on any card
  • 5H - Swap Hands: If the 5 of Hearts is played, all players pass their hand to the next player, in the direction of play
  • 7 - Under: If a 7 is played, the player must play a card with a value equal to or below a 7 i.e. 2,3,4,5,6,7. A 7 may also be played on an 8 or a 9
  • 8 - Reverse: If an 8 is played, the direction of play is reversed. Playing multiple 8's stacks the effect i.e. 2x 8s continue the same direction.
    • N.B. In a 2-player game, 8s skip the other player.
  • 10 - Burn: If a 10 is played, the current discard pile is removed from the game, and the player plays again. A 10 may be played on any card except a 7.

How to Play

  • When 2 or more players arrive in the lobby, a blue button will appear, which can be clicked to start a game.
    • N.B. Any players arriving after a game has start will be placed in a queue until the game is won or restarted
  • Before the game commences, a player may swap cards from the hand (bottom row of cards) to their face-up cards (middle row of cards). To do so, they must click the cards they want to swap (so they appear in the bottom-right section of the screen), and then click the "Swap cards" button
    • N.B. A player doesn't have to swap the cards evenly i.e. 2-for-1, however they won't be able to continue until their hand and face-up cards hold 3 cards each.
  • Once a player is ready, the must click the "Ready" button, and wait for all other players to be ready. When all players are ready, gameplay will begin, and the Game Log will display a message describing whose turn it is.
  • To play a card, a player must click it, so that it appears in the bottom-right section of the screen. When they have selected all cards to play, they must click the "Play Cards" button
